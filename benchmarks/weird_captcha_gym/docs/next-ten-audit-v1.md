# Next-ten benchmark audit (dashboard positions 17–26)

This cohort is audited against the interaction-puzzle field notes, not against
the weaker standard of “the scripted green path passes.” The binding question
is whether a sharp, knowledgeable model can answer from one or two static
frames. If it can, the mechanic is not a useful computer-use benchmark.

## Acceptance gates

Every mechanic in this cohort must satisfy all of the following before human
review:

1. Initial, active, failure, recovery, solved, and explicit PASS states are
   visually distinct and captured.
2. The rendered geometry, pointer hit geometry, state transitions, and grader
   replay use the same coordinate contract.
3. A failure issues either a real recoverable state or a visibly fresh
   challenge; no silent reset, phantom collision, or decorative-only control.
4. The scripted solver uses real browser input. Reading ground truth is allowed
   only to wire a deterministic evidence path; it is not evidence of human
   solvability.
5. At least three fresh seeds pass generator invariants, server grading,
   independent grading, task verification, browser-console checks, and visual
   inspection.
6. Human-review state remains pending. This audit cannot self-approve a puzzle.

## Completed audit outcome

The five weak concepts were rebuilt rather than cosmetically repaired:

- `surreal_apple_on_tree_grid` is now **Parallax Orchard**. All apparent
  contacts coincide in the head-on projection; the replay requires a 96°+
  camera baseline, 155°+ cumulative orbit travel, four view sectors, real
  apple hit geometry, and physical basket drags.
- `cursor_lens_reveal` is now **Polarized Palimpsest**. The replay requires a
  broad local scan, multiple polarizer changes, five ordered moving holds,
  sustained tracking samples, and a recoverable dropped hold.
- `modifier_stack_image_grid` is now **Kinetic Restoration Press**. Three
  transient films must be remembered, the physical modules must be placed in
  reverse order and switched to their inverse, and each artifact must cross a
  continuous three-gate rail.
- `board_game_captcha` is now **Gyroscopic Tilt Board**. The browser and grader
  share a deterministic 50 ms acceleration, friction, speed-cap, circle-wall
  collision, hazard, ordered-lamp, and goal-cup simulation.
- `occlusion_shell_swindle` now exposes every under-cover carrier result through
  a timed cursor-held physical peephole. A missed window produces a visible
  rewind path. The old arbitrary hidden transfer and `IDENTITY RELAY` answer
  label are gone.

The retained mechanics also received a presentation audit:

- Shadow Crime Lab and CRAFTCHA were retained because their causal and
  long-horizon contracts already satisfy the benchmark principle.
- Ribbon Switchboard no longer exposes its internal coverage/audit table or
  layout-clearance diagnostics on the player surface.
- Magnetic Stripe Purgatory now relies on the physical arrow, card/reader
  badges, and reader feedback instead of a three-step procedural tutorial.
- Trajectory Catcher no longer names hidden paths as ballistic, sine, or cubic;
  the player must infer the live vector from motion.
