## Cohort-wide gates

- A passing trajectory contains no intentional miss, collision, invalid roll,
  wrong tool, reset, or replay unless the task itself makes that event
  unavoidable and visible.
- No task passes because it performed a ceremonial control action.  Evidence
  quotas must correspond to information or state genuinely needed to solve.
- Visible render geometry, pointer hit testing, local dynamics, submitted
  transcript, live server grading, and exported verifier replay agree.
- Difficulty is increased through coupled state, active sensing, temporal
  observation, 3-D/spatial control, prediction, or sustained motor work—not
  through smaller targets, shorter windows, unexplained instructions, or
  hidden rejection.
- Changed generators demonstrate structural variation and reachability across
  fresh seeds.  Empty claims, stale challenge/task identities, forged geometry,
  and shortcut terminal payloads are rejected.
- Every mechanic receives a real invalid/fresh-failure run and a separate clean
  ordinary-input pass through browser, live server, direct grader, and exported
  verifier, with console checks and 1280×720 evidence.
- Solution films are recorded only after task-facing hashes are frozen.  Film
  pacing may expose interaction; it may not alter thresholds or simplify a
  task.
- The nine `pending` and two `revision_requested` states remain unchanged until
  the user reviews the final interactions and films.
